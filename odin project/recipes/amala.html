Amala and Ewedu with Gbegiri

Ingredients

  • Yam flour
  • Water
  • Brown beans
  • Palm oil
  • Salt
  • Crayfish
  • maggi
  • Beef
  • Ewedu
  • Assorted meats

How to prepare ewedu

  1. Allow the frozen jute leave to thaw then blend till it's semi-smooth.
  2. Pour blend into a pot and bring to boil, then add crayfish, salt, Iru ,stock cube and egusi (if using)
  3. Stir well and allow it simmer for 10 minutes until the soup begins to bubble and draw.
  4. take off the heat and serve.

how to prepare Amala

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Gradually stir in yam flour.
  3. Keep stirring until it becomes smooth and firm.
  4. Mold into balls and serve warm.
